Impact and Urgency Definitions


When logging a ticket, please review and select the most correct Impact and Urgency that pertains to your issue

Impact :

- Low Business impact to Intel Corporation (Nuisance - Workaround)

- Medium Business impact to Intel Corporation (One worker outage. Painful workaround)

- High Business impact to Intel Corporation (System down. Business screeches to a halt)


Urgency :

- Low: Could address in a medium to long time frame.

- Medium : Can be addressed soon, but other business activity can carry forward in the short term.

- High : Must be addressed now, the business need requires immediate action or attention.
