Contract Manager Features

The contract manage tab defaults to the Approval View which gives you the ability to send for approval or approve ,and/or send for revision or self revise strategies. You may search for strategies or tables by names, expand open tables for quick views of deals, and view the contract in numerous ways, such as the overlaps, contract as a whole, all the deals within the contrct, and cost test and meet comp information.

In addition to sending for approval you are able to change the contract acceptance as well as copy or delete the contract.
