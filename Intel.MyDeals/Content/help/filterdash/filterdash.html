Filtering Dashboard

You may filter by customer and/or date range. This will refresh your dashboard and show only information with the applied filters.

*Note that the filters also apply when searching or creating contracts. If a specific customers are selected, search results will show only results for those selected customer.

TIP: Having filters applied helps with performance.

You are able to filter your MyDeals dashboard by using the filters in the top right-hand corner of the MyDeals Home page.
