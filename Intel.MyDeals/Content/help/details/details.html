Contract Details

When creating a new contract you are required to fill out details about the contract such as customer, name, dates, customer acceptance, and C2A#. The contract details page also provides information about the status' and history with the contract timeline. You are also able to add attachment and make notes.

TIP: If you would like to get your deals pre-approved you may keep your contract in Customer Acceptance Pending and send in for approval. They will be pre-approved and once the acceptance from the customer has been received you may come and switch to Customer Acceptance, Accepted and your deals will go Active. *Note: this does not apply to Tender deals please see Tenders for more information.
