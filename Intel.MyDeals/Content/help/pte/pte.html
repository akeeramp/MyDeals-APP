Pricing Table Editor Features

After you have named, selected the deal type, and setup your table auto defaults you will be brought to the Pricing Table Editor. Here you will enter your deal information starting with entering contract products. You will notice that your auto defaulted selections will pre-populate in the grid upon entry. When you selected your Table deal type the web spreadsheet template is optimized for that deal type.

At the top of your table you have the ability to edit your Pricing Strategy or Table names. You also have a menu bar with various options.

TIP: By keeping less than 200 rows per table will improve application performance
