Excluding Products

There are two ways to exclude products when entering high level products (such as for Vol Tier or Program deal entry) into My Deals, either by cutting and pasting or typing a product into the Pricing Table Editor cells or by making a selection from the Product Selector.

TIP: Using the Product Selector for making exclusions help show you which products are missing CAP.
