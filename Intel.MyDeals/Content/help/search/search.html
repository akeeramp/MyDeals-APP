Search

There a two places that you are able to search from the homepage. One is within the search widget using the filters that are selected from the top right hand corner. You may search for either contract name/number, pricing strategy, pricing table, or deal number.

TIP: Searching by the filtered search is faster than searching globally

You are able to filter your MyDeals dashboard by using the filters in the top right-hand corner of the MyDeals Home page.
