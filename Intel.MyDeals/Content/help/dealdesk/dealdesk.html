Deal Desk

The main feature of the dashboard is the Deal Desk widget. This widget shows you the contracts by their status'. You are able to sort the columns by clicking the header of the column you would like to sort, or filter by status by selecting the lefthand side. You are also able to expand to see a summary of the Pricing Strategies within the contract.

TIP: You may use the favorites star to mark contracts you would like to track.

You are able to filter your MyDeals dashboard by using the filters in the top right-hand corner of the MyDeals Home page.
