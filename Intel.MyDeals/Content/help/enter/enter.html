Entering Products

There are two ways to enter products into My Deals, either by cutting and pasting into the Pricing Table Editor cells or by selecting from the Product Selector.

When adding products for KIT deals, the products will be laid out in separate rows for you (see image below).

For Vol Tier & Program you may also enter at high levels such as DT Core i3 and exclude products using the exclude column.

TIP: You may cut/paste multiple products into a cell which will copy all the additional info to all products entered. For ECAPs the products will be entered into their own deals for you. You may cut/paste syntaxes such as , / or & ().
