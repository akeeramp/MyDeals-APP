Grouping Exclusions

The My Deals tool automatically runs comprehensive Cost Testing. In some cases users may choose to exclude some deals from the auto grouping in certain cases. When viewing the groupings users are able to select which deals they would like to exclude. A reason will be required if selections are made.

TIP: The grouping exclusions pop up shows you all the active deals for that customer in the tool.

Grouping Exclusions Page

Click to view all the deals and select the View from the Exclude Deal Group column.

Exclusion Pop up

A pop up window will show the deals that are being cost tested across the other contracts in the system. It will show the product in the light blue bar, then below the contracts where those deals are located. It will also display the deals that overlap but are not part of the Cost Test.
