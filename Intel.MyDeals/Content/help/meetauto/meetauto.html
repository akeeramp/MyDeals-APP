Meet Comp Auto Population

Users are able to visit the Meet Comp tab after their first set of products are entered and validated. The Meet Comp tab is used to process and manage Meet Comp attributes by product and/or by deal. Upon visiting the tab the My Deals tool will Auto Populate any saved Meet Comp attributes for each product for that customer.

Users have the ability to overwrite and save the updated data for population the next time a contract is created.

Users may enter the data for the product and it will apply to that product as it exists in all deals within the current contract. Users also have the ability to enter deal specific data when expanding to the deal level.

TIP: Each customized table will be saved for the customer and a separate one for each deal type.
