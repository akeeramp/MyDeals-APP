Approve and Revise

You are able to select all (entire contract) or some of the pricing strategies for sending for approval. Customer Acceptance will determine the deal status after approvals have been made. When selecting as Customer Accepted if you have not entered a C2A ID#, or attached a copy or URL of the accepted contract you will prompted to enter this information. The deals will not go active until this information has been provided.

TIP: When submitting make sure to send an email to your approver so they know there is something for approval.

Approval with Pending Customer Acceptance

You may enter and have your deals approved by the approver before customer acceptance allowing for earlier deal entry. When customer acceptance has been received you may change the status and any approvals done will got active.

Approval with Customer Acceptance

When entering deals with Customer Acceptance any that get approved go active unless a hold has been put on them.

Approval with Customer Acceptance not required

In few cases Customer Acceptance is not required, when selected deals go active after approval unless a hold has been put on them
