Contract Navigator

When either creating a new contract or navigating around an existing one the contract navigator makes it easy to add, edit, delete, view status', and/or search for strategies or tables. The tabs at the top help you flow the contract entry process. Starting with entering deals, managing meet comp, and submitting or revising strategies. An additional side menu is available for editing autofill defaults, deleting or adding additional items.

TIP: You may use the icon to hide the Navigator for optimal screen real estate when entering or viewing deals in the tabs to the right.
