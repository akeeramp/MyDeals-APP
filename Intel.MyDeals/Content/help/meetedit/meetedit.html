Meet Comp Editing / Adding

You are able to add new values for any Meet Comp data. Meet Comp data is auto populated with your customers meet comp data, if it does not exist then it will populate with any general product meet comp information. You may override this information which will automatically save for the next time and auto populate in your next contract.

TIP: Any new Meet Comp data that you edit or add will automatically be saved in your current customer contract and used in future contracts for that customer that you create. *Note: This will not update any existing contracts in the system and will only apply the new data to new contracts.
