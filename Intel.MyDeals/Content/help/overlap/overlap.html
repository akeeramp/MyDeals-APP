Overlapping Deals Check

My Deals will show you any deals that overlap with other tools within the tool. There are a few different places where you may review the overlapping deals.

TIP: The My Deals tool will prevent you from submitting your contract if any overlapping deals exist that meet the hard stop requirements

Pricing Table Editor

When entering your deal information you may check to see if there are any overlapping deals. Make sure to save an validate the inputted data before selecting the overlapping check.

Deal Editor

You may check for overlapping deals in the Deal Editor. Make sure to save and validate if you made any additional changes after entering from the Pricing Table Editor.

Contract Manager

Using the Overlapping Deals link from the Contract Manager. You may also click the open in a window button to pop out the information and navigate throughout the tool with the information in the pop out.
