Contract and Deals Views

You are able to view the contract in a contract form as well as to view all the deals that exist in the contract

TIP: If the person entering the deals structured them to match the customer contract this is an easy way to compare the My Deals contract with the customer contract

Contract View

Use this view to compare your customer contract with your My Deals contract. It scructure by strategy then tables. You can also see all the ammedments made to the contract.

Deal View

This view allows you to see all the deals in the contract as well as filter by deal type.
