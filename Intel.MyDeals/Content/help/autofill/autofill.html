Autofill Defaults

When adding a new Pricing Table you are asked to set your autofill defaults. These are selected as your start but may be overwritten in the Pricing Table Editor at any time. After selecting you may also reopen to change the settings for any new deals entered in the spreadsheet. Click the Auto Default icon on the menu bar of the Pricing Table Editor.

TIP: When change your Auto Defaults settings from the Pricing Table Editor any new deals entered will populate with the updated info without overwriting your previous ones.
