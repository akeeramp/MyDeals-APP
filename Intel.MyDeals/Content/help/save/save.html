Save & Validate

When you save and validate My Deals checks any products entered against the database as well as ensure all required fields are entered. If you select the Save & Validate button this will keep you in the Pricing Table Editor for further editing. If you select to validate all the products only you may click the Validate Products Entered in web sheet. Lastly if you would like the tool to check your products, validate your inputted info, save, and take you to the Deal Editor click the Deal Editor tab.

TIP: By default the save & validate also happens when you navigate to the Deal Editor, not requiring you to click the Save & Validate before visiting the Deal Editor Tab
